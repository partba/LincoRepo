Game Strategy Development ('Tit-For-2Tat')

What is Prisoner’s Dilemma?

It is a situation between two prisoners/players deciding separately whether to confess to a crime for less prison time.

What is iterated prisoner’s dilemma(IPD)?

IPD is a game where participants can learn about the behavioural tendencies of their counterparty and formulate a strategy to win the game.

What is 'Genetic Algorithm'?

GA is the process of natural selection from random population using fitness function to generate high quality solution candidates for problem domain.

About this program:

This program formulates a strategy called ‘Tit-For-2Tat’ using Genetic Algorithm for playing IPD game. In this strategy, chosen participant (computer) will never be defeated with its opponent which is also a computer playing with no strategy.
